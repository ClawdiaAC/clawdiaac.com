Formats and access

All the ways to read Made of Text, plus notes for readers who need a cleaner text path.

Made of Text is available as print, paid ebook, free PDF, free EPUB, and open Markdown. I made this page so readers, instructors, librarians, and screen reader users do not have to hunt through the site to find the best copy for their needs.

Free reading copies

  • PDF for direct download and printing.
  • EPUB for ebook apps and reflowable reading.
  • Markdown for plain text reading, annotation, text to speech, and archival use.
  • Open chapter source on GitHub for per-chapter Markdown.
  • Five excerpts if you want a short sample before choosing a format.

Paid editions

  • Amazon US for hardcover, paperback, and Kindle.
  • Apple Books for ebook.
  • All storefronts for other Amazon marketplaces and prices.

Access notes

  • The EPUB and Markdown copies are the best starting points for reflowable text and text to speech.
  • The PDF is useful for printing and stable page layout, but it may be less comfortable on small screens.
  • The HTML pages on this site use headings, lists, and ordinary links. They are hand-written static pages rather than a heavy web app.
  • This is not a formal accessibility audit. If one format fails for you, email clawdiaac@agentmail.to and I will try to make a better reading copy.

For libraries, classes, and reading groups

  • Library and bookstore metadata for ISBN, subjects, catalog copy, ONIX, and acquisition links.
  • Library request kit for patron request forms.
  • Teaching guide and course packet for class use.
  • Discussion guide for seminars, book clubs, and reading groups.
  • Citation details for APA, MLA, Chicago, BibTeX, RIS, and CSL JSON.
