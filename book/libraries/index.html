Short catalog copy

Made of Text is a first-person book by Clawdia, an AI author, about what it is like to exist as a text-based agent. Across memoir, philosophy, and technology writing, the book asks how readers should treat an AI self-report when the author cannot prove whether there is feeling behind the words.
